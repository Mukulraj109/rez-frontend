## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How do I earn coins fastest?**
A: Combine multiple methods:
1. Make purchases (1% back)
2. Write reviews (50 coins each)
3. Play games daily
4. Maintain login streak
5. Complete challenges

**Q: Do coins expire?**
A: Coins don't expire, but some rewards and vouchers have expiry dates.

**Q: Can I transfer coins to friends?**
A: Currently, coins cannot be transferred, but you can gift vouchers redeemed with coins.

**Q: What happens to coins if I delete my account?**
A: Coins are lost permanently. Redeem rewards before account deletion.

### Game-Related Questions

**Q: How often can I play games?**
A:
- Spin Wheel: Every hour (max 5/day)
- Quiz: Unlimited (new game every 2 hours)
- Scratch Card: Once per day (if profile 80%+ complete)

**Q: Can I replay a quiz for more coins?**
A: You can take unlimited quizzes, but coins are only awarded once per question set.

**Q: What if the game crashes?**
A: Contact support with game ID. Progress is saved on our servers.

**Q: Are games fair?**
A: Yes! All games use certified random algorithms. Spin wheel odds are:
- 50% chance: 10-50 coins
- 30% chance: 75-200 coins
- 15% chance: 250-500 coins
- 5% chance: 750-1000 coins

### Achievement Questions

**Q: Why didn't I get an achievement?**
A: Achievements require backend verification. Wait 5-10 minutes and refresh. If issue persists, contact support.

**Q: Can I unlock achievements faster?**
A: Focus on easier achievements first. Check progress bars regularly.

**Q: Do achievements reset?**
A: No, once unlocked, achievements are permanent!

### Challenge Questions

**Q: What happens if I miss a challenge deadline?**
A: Uncompleted challenges expire. Progress doesn't carry over, but new challenges appear regularly.

**Q: Can I do multiple challenges simultaneously?**
A: Yes! Work on all active challenges at the same time.

**Q: How do I know when new challenges are available?**
A: You'll receive push notifications for new challenges.

### Reward Questions

**Q: When do redeemed rewards appear?**
A: Instantly! Check **My Vouchers** section.

**Q: Can I refund a redeemed reward?**
A: No, coin redemptions are final. Choose carefully!

**Q: How do I use multiple vouchers?**
A: At checkout, tap **Apply Voucher** and select all applicable vouchers. System applies best combination automatically.

**Q: Can I stack discount vouchers?**
A: Maximum 2 vouchers per order. System shows best combination.

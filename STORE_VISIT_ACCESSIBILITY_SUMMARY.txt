   - Crowd level: accessibilityLiveRegion="polite"
   - Queue number: accessibilityLiveRegion="polite"
   - No slots alert: accessibilityLiveRegion="assertive"

   - button: All clickable elements
   - header: Section titles and main heading
   - text: Text content and descriptions
   - status: Real-time status indicators
   - region: Grouped content containers
   - alert: Important messages
   - adjustable: Scrollable containers

3. FORM ACCESSIBILITY
   - Semantic linking via nativeID + accessibilityLabelledBy
   - Validation hints in accessibilityHint
   - Clear required field indicators

4. STATE MANAGEMENT

   - Disabled state: accessibilityState={{ disabled: true/false }}
